Poha

Category: Breakfast | Cuisine: Indian (Madhya Pradesh Special)

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small potato, peeled and diced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Rinse poha in water, drain, and let it sit for 5 minutes. Add salt and sugar, and mix gently.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle. Then add peanuts and sauté until golden.
  3. Step 3: Add chopped onions, green chilies, and diced potatoes. Cook until potatoes are soft.
  4. Step 4: Add turmeric powder, mix, and then gently fold in the poha. Cook for 2-3 minutes on low flame.
  5. Step 5: Turn off the heat, add lemon juice, and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use thick poha to prevent it from becoming mushy.
  • Add a handful of grated coconut for an enhanced flavor.
  • Serve with a side of jalebi for a traditional Indori breakfast.
