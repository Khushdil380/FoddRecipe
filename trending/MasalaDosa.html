Masala Dosa

Category: Breakfast | Cuisine: South Indian

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Moderate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (for dosa batter)
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 4 medium-sized boiled potatoes
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6-8 hours. Grind them into a smooth batter and let it ferment overnight.
  2. Step 2: Prepare the masala by heating oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, chopped onion, green chilies, and turmeric. Mix with mashed boiled potatoes. Add salt and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan and spread a ladleful of dosa batter to form a thin, crispy crepe.
  4. Step 4: Place a spoonful of the potato masala in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Tips & Tricks

  • Ensure the batter is well-fermented for a crispier dosa.
  • Use a non-stick pan or cast iron skillet for best results.
  • Serve dosas immediately to enjoy their crispiness.
