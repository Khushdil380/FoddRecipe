Aloo Tikki Chaat

Category: Snack | Cuisine: Indian

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (for binding)
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (whisked)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp mint chutney
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Onion, finely chopped (for garnish)
  • Pomegranate seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine the boiled and mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, cumin powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Step 2: Add cornflour to the mixture and knead into a smooth dough-like consistency.
  3. Step 3: Divide the mixture into small portions and shape each into a flat round patty (tikki).
  4. Step 4: Heat oil in a pan and fry the tikkis on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides. Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  5. Step 5: To assemble the chaat, place the tikkis on a serving plate.
  6. Step 6: Drizzle with whisked yogurt, tamarind chutney, and mint chutney.
  7. Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, chopped onions, and pomegranate seeds (optional).
  8. Step 8: Serve immediately and enjoy the crispy and flavorful Aloo Tikki Chaat!

Tips & Tricks

  • Ensure the tikkis are crispy by frying them on medium heat.
  • If you want a tangy flavor, add extra tamarind chutney.
  • You can also add sev (crispy chickpea noodles) for extra crunch.
