Paratha

Category: Breakfast | Cuisine: Indian

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee (for frying)
  • 1/2 cup water (as needed for dough)
  • Optional fillings: boiled potatoes, paneer, grated radish, or spinach
  • Spices for filling (if used): cumin, coriander, chili powder, and garam masala

Instructions

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, mix wheat flour, salt, and water to knead a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Step 2: If using a filling, mix the desired filling (potatoes, paneer, etc.) with spices and keep it ready.
  3. Step 3: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle, place the filling in the center, and fold the edges to seal.
  4. Step 4: Gently roll the filled dough ball into a flat circle, ensuring the filling doesn't spill out.
  5. Step 5: Heat a skillet and place the rolled paratha on it. Cook on medium flame, applying oil or ghee on both sides, until golden brown spots appear.
  6. Step 6: Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or chutney.

Tips & Tricks

  • Ensure the dough is soft and pliable for easy rolling.
  • Adjust the spice level of the filling as per taste.
  • Use ghee for frying to enhance the flavor of the paratha.
