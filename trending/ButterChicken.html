Butter Chicken

Category: Main Course | Cuisine: Indian

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Marination Time: 1 hour

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 2 hours

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

For Marination:

  • 500g chicken (boneless or bone-in)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

For the Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  2. Step 2: Heat a pan and grill the marinated chicken pieces until slightly charred and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: In the same pan, add butter and sauté onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Step 4: Add tomato puree, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Step 5: Add the grilled chicken pieces to the gravy, mix well, and cook for 10 minutes on low heat.
  6. Step 6: Add fresh cream, kasuri methi, and garam masala. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Tips & Tricks

  • Marinate the chicken overnight for deeper flavors.
  • Use fresh cream for a richer texture and taste.
  • Serve with buttered naan for an authentic experience.
